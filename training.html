Ganesh Chaturthi

Son Of Lord Shiva

"Ganesh Chaturthi: a vibrant, joyous celebration of Lord Ganesha’s birth, marked by elaborate decorations, energetic processions, and community unity, reflecting rich cultural traditions and a spirit of devotion. Ganesh Chaturthi celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha, the elephant-headed deity known for wisdom and removing obstacles. The festival honors his arrival on Earth, symbolizing new beginnings, prosperity, and the removal of hurdles in life. It involves vibrant rituals, community gatherings, and elaborate decorations, reflecting devotion and joy."
